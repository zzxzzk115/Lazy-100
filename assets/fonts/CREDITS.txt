Built-in font: Fusion Pixel Font (缝合像素字体), 10px monospaced, zh_hans variant.
Author:  TakWolf and contributors
Source:  https://github.com/TakWolf/fusion-pixel-font
Release: 2026.07.01
License: SIL Open Font License 1.1 -- see fusion-pixel-OFL.txt

Used as Lazy-100's built-in font; linked into the binary (embed/lazy100.rc on
Windows, embed/lazy100.S elsewhere) and rasterized at runtime with stb_truetype
at its native 10px size. Monospaced so code and the tracker align in columns.
